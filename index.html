About The Event

CERVMUN proudly marks its tenth edition, a milestone shaped by a decade of legacy, dedication, and the pursuit of diplomacy. Over the years, the conference has grown into a platform that not only encourages academic excellence but also fosters values of cooperation, critical thinking, and global awareness. Each edition has built upon the last, carrying forward traditions while introducing fresh perspectives that keep the spirit of CERVMUN alive and evolving. Reaching this landmark tenth year is a testament to the commitment of its organizers, participants, and supporters who have continually contributed to its success.

Across three days, students from diverse schools will come together to debate pressing global issues, showcase their talent, and embrace the spirit of leadership. Beyond the committees and discussions, CERVMUN provides an opportunity to form lasting friendships and connections, making it not just a conference but a meaningful experience that empowers the diplomats of tomorrow.

Words from the Secretariat

CERVMUN has been a space where I’ve grown, challenged myself, and discovered strengths I didn’t know I had. But above all, it’s been a place where I’ve built friendships that I now hold close to my heart.

I hope that over these two days of debate, you not only push yourselves intellectually but also discover just how much you are capable of. May every argument build your confidence, and may this experience not only enrich your academic journey but also become a memory you carry for life.”
