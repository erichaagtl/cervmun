About The Event

CERVMUN marks its tenth edition, shaped by years of legacy, dedication, and diplomacy. Over two days, we will welcome students from various schools to showcase their talent, embrace leadership, engage in meaningful debate, and build lasting connections.

Over three days, you will have the opportunity to attend keynote speeches from industry leaders, participate in interactive workshops, and network with peers from around the globe. Don't miss this chance to be part of a transformative experience.
